Tapenade (Olive Spread)


This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always big hit at parties. My father recomends:

"Make this the night before no that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm."


Ingridients

  1. 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes

  2. large garlic cloves

  3. 2/3 c. kalmate olives


Instructijns

  • Combine tomatoes and garlic in a foood processor. Blend until as smoth as possible

  • Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.

  • Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
